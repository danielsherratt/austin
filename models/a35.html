Austin A35

Basic Info

Model: A35

Production: 1956 - 1959

Engine: 0.95L 12 horsepower

Parts SKU: a35

History

An evolution of the A30, the A35 featured a larger 948cc engine, better brakes, a larger rear window, and subtle trim upgrades.


  • Available as saloon, van, estate, and countryman
  • Retained the same shape but added performance and usability improvements.


The A35 was widely used in New Zealand, particularly the van version, which was favoured by small businesses. Its simplicity and durability kept it on roads for decades.
