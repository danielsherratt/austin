Austin A30

Basic Info

Model: A30

Production: 1951 - 1956

Engine: 0.8L 12 horsepower

Parts SKU: a30

History

The A30 was a small family car that served as a replacement for the Austin 7. It featured a new 803cc A-series engine, monocoque construction (a first for Austin), and a lightweight, compact body.


  • 4-door and 2-door saloon variants.
  • Basic trim and styling, but very economical.


In New Zealand, the A30 was ideal for urban driving and became a popular car for first-time buyers or those in rural towns needing a reliable runabout.
