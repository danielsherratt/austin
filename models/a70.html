Austin A70

Basic Info

Model: A70

Production: 1948 - 1954

Engine: 0.7L 12 horsepower

Parts SKU: a70

History

The A70 series catered to the mid-size executive market. It used a larger 2.2L engine and was available in Hampshire and later Hereford versions.


Key Variants:

  • Hampshire (1948–1950) – Angular design, split windscreen, and generous interior space.
  • Box Saloon – More refined, enclosed body introduced in the late 1920s.
  • Hereford (1950–1954) – Rounded bodywork, enhanced suspension.


These were solid, comfortable cruisers, aimed at professionals and upper-middle-class families. Some A70s were used as taxis, and a few utility and pickup variants were made. New Zealand saw modest numbers, mostly for rural professionals or light commercial use
